Thank you for using Charon!
To Get Started use `${settings.prefix}settings`
Your Discord's default settings should look like this
```{ prefix: '-',
  modLogChannel: 'mod-log',
  modRole: 'Moderator',
  adminRole: 'Administrator',
  systemNotice: 'true',
  welcomeChannel: 'welcome',
  welcomeMessage: 'Welcome {{user}} to the Discord!',
  welcomeEnabled: 'false',
  goodbyeChannel: 'goodbye',
  goodbyeMessage: 'Goodbye {{user}} see you later!',
  goodbyeEnabled: 'false',
  autoRole: 'member',
  autoRoleEnabled: 'false' }```
To configure these settings simply use `${settings.prefix}set` *<view/get/edit> <key> <value>*
Example: ```${settings.prefix}set edit modLogChannel myModLogsyeet```

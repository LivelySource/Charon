_**Moderation**_\n
`${settings.prefix}ban` **Bans a rule breaking user.** __Usage__: *${settings.prefix}ban <@user> <reason>*
`${settings.prefix}banlist` **Looks up your server's banlist** __Usage__: *${settings.prefix}banlist*
`${settings.prefix}giverole` **Give a selected user a role** __Usage__: *${settings.prefix}giverole <@user> <role>*
`${settings.prefix}takerole` **Take a user's role** __Usage__: *${settings.prefix}takerole <@user> <role>*
`${settings.prefix}id` **Looks up a user's ID** __Usage__: *${settings.prefix}id <@user>*
`${settings.prefix}userinfo` **Gives some useful user information** __Usage__: *${settings.prefix}userinfo <@user>*
`${settings.prefix}purge` **Purges chat messages** __Usage__: *${settings.prefix}purge <amount>(2-1000)*
